2. Fill the positions


  • Each column is the result of each race
    Fill your team position to calculate the total

  • If your team palette is selected in Input Format, you can click on the position to fill
    You can also drag to fill

  • If "Key" is selected, you can fill the positions with your keyboard by entering the key you choosed in "Keyboard shortcut"

  • Map is optional

  • you can toggle the lock for each race result

  • You need to manually fill the first race positions to create the sample

  • After first race positions are filled, paste the first race result screenshot

  • Click on the image, then click on "Create Sample"

  • If the sample creation is succeeded, you can paste the screenshot of remaining race results screen with finished position (not the total score position)
